Privacy Policy

At Signature Bites, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains what type of information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it secure when you visit our website or place an order with us.

Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal information when you place an order, fill out a form, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us directly.

This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information

This information is used to process orders, confirm deliveries, provide customer support, and improve our services.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your payment details are handled securely and are not stored permanently on our website.

Non Personal Identification Information

We may collect non personal information when you visit our website. This helps us understand how users interact with our site.

This may include:

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Date and time of visit

This information helps us improve website performance, design, and user experience.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences.

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function properly.

Data Protection and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Only authorized staff members have access to personal data, and they are required to keep this information confidential.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data. You may also request correction or deletion of your information at any time.

If you wish to update or remove your data, you can contact us directly.

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites such as payment gateways or social media platforms.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Signature Bites may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

We encourage users to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect their information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

You can visit our Contact Us page for any queries or support.
